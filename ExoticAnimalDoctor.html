Volume 1
Welcome to Gekko Animal Hospital: the go-to veterinary clinic for exotic pets! Whether your animal companion has scales, a shell, numerous legs, or sharp fangs, the staff at Gekko is here to help. From a tortoise with a split shell to anemic tarantulas and burned snakes, the staff goes to great lengths to care for the critters that require extra special care.

Volume 2
Kanna Nozomi always wanted to work at a veterinary clinic, but when he finally set foot on his dream career path, he developed an allergy to dogs, which cost him his new job. Undeterred, Kanna searched for a vet who specialized in exotic animals, meaning anything but dogs and cats, and found the Gekko Animal Hospital. Dr. Gekko is a woman of clipped words but a wealth of information about exotic animals. Dr. Gekko quickly sets about training him on how to work with the wilder side of the animal kingdom. But when a new case requires a totally original treatment method, have Dr. Gekko and her eager new hire met their match?

Welcome to Gekko Animal Hospital, where the staff treats the kinds of patients most vets won't dare touch! Whether your pet has fur, scales, talons, or razor-sharp fangs, this dedicated team is ready to help. From a tortoise with a cracked shell to an anemic tarantula or a snake recovering from burns, they work tirelessly to provide expert care and heartfelt compassion to some of the world’s most extraordinary animal companions.
