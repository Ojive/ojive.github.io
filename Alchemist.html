Volume 1
SHE HOPED FOR CITY LIVING BUT GOT A ONE-WAY TICKET TO A TROPICAL ISLAND INSTEAD?!

No one can deny that Freya is a prodigy. After she graduates from the Royal Institute of Alchemy, she wants to live it up in a workshop in the big city. Little does she know, she’s about to be assigned to a backwater island far to the south! This seaside getaway isn’t looking too hot, though—infrastructure is breaking down, and villagers are falling ill. Shortly after arriving, Freya meets Lilil, the guardian deity of the island, and at her request (and with the promise of an all-access fur-petting pass), the alchemical genius decides to help restore the island to its former glory. It may not be the cushy postgrad life she had in mind, but she’s still gonna make the most of it!
