Author: Takibi Amamori
Artist: Itachi
Company: Seven Seas
Official Site

Note: I started working on this series as of volume 3.


Volume 3
As an avid fan of rom-coms, Nukumizu Kazuhiko can't help but be drawn to the joys and pain of romance, but he never suspected that he'd go from being a spectator to being spectated! With rumors spreading that he's in a relationship with Yanami, Nukumizu must make a choice whether to retreat back to the shadows or continue supporting his friend. At the same time, Yanami has still yet to confront her childhood friend about the feelings she's been holding onto for years. A heroine can still lose with her head held high!

Volume 4
Is there any excuse for a high school girl to be going out alone with her crush who already has a girlfriend? That's exactly what Nukumizu and Yanami are determined to find out. When they spot Yakishio alone with her childhood friend Ayano, the two team up with his girlfriend Chihaya to figure out what on earth is going on. He might already be taken, but Lemon's not yet ready to let go of her feelings. Could she be planning a boyfriend heist?
